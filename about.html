About Pulse

Started by a physiotherapist tired of watching gym injuries walk into her clinic.

Pulse opened in 2017 with one simple fix: put the trainer and the physiotherapist in the same building, working from the same notes.

Our Mission

Fewer injuries, better numbers, longer careers in the gym.

Most gym injuries come from bad programming, not bad luck — a plan that ignores old injuries or pushes volume too fast. We built Pulse so that doesn't happen: every trainer has a physiotherapist's notes before writing your program.

That's also why we track more than weight lifted. Mobility scores, sleep, and recovery markers all factor into how your program changes month to month.

What Guides Us

Three principles behind every program.

01

Assessment Before Ambition

No program starts without a movement screening — goals come second to what your body can safely do today.

02

Recovery Is Part of the Plan

Rest days and mobility work are scheduled, not left to chance, because burnout undoes progress fast.

03

Numbers Over Guesswork

Body composition scans and lift logs replace "how do you feel" as the main measure of progress.
